QUESTION 1:- What is SEO and Explain the importance of SEO ?

ANSWER:-

SEO(Search Engine Optimization) is a set of strategies and techniques aimed at improving the visibility and ranking of a website or web page in search engine results pages. The primary goal of SEO is to increase organic traffic to a website by optimizing various elements such as content,keywords,meta tags,and site sructure.
